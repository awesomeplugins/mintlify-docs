# How to Remove a Website from Your Awesomeplugins License

If you need to switch or remove a website associated with your Awesome plugin license, follow these steps to efficiently manage your license allocation.

## Step 1: Log in to Your Awesomeplugins Account

1. Visit the Awesomeplugins website and log in to your account.
2. Navigate to the dashboard or section specifically related to your purchased licenses.

## Step 2: Access License Management

1. Locate the specific plugin license that needs modification.
2. Look for an option such as "Manage License," "License Details," or similar.

## Step 3: View Website Allocation

1. Within the license management section, you will find a list of websites currently associated with your license.
2. Identify the website you wish to remove.

## Step 4: Remove Website

1. Find an option or button labeled "Remove" or "Deactivate" next to the website you want to detach from the license.
2. Click on this option to initiate the removal process.

## Step 5: Confirm Removal

1. A confirmation prompt may appear to confirm the removal of the website.
2. Confirm the action to proceed with detaching the website from your license.

## Troubleshooting Tips:

- If you encounter any issues during the removal process, double-check that you have the necessary permissions.
- Ensure that you are logged in with the account associated with the license.
- Contact our support team if you face any difficulties or need further assistance.

## Additional Notes:

- Removing a website from your license does not affect the plugin's functionality on other active websites.
- Ensure that the website you are removing is not actively using the plugin, as this may disrupt its functionality.

Congratulations! You have successfully removed a website from your Awesome plugin license. If you have any questions or encounter issues, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
